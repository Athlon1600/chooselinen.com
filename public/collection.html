I do not create stock.

All items listed in my Etsy shop are produced only after they have been ordered. Every step of creating my items such as designing the pattern, pre-washing the fabric, cutting and sewing takes place in my home in Florida. I do my best to ship your order within one to two weeks.

I also accept custom orders. If your measurements do not match sizes in my size chart, or you prefer some adjustments please do not hesitate to contact me.
